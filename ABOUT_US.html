Terrain

In 2023, a passionate and environmentally conscious college student named "MD Sharik" initiated an inspiring project called "Terrain" with the mission to protect the beauty of the Earth and raise awareness about its astonishing diversity. Recognizing the urgent need to address environmental issues and foster a sense of stewardship for our planet, Sharik envisioned a project that would captivate people's hearts and minds, igniting a global movement towards preserving the natural wonders of Earth.
